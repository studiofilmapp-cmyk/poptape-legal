Moafilm Support

Moafilm is an app for recording short videos and opening them together as one film at a set time. If you have a question or run into a problem, please reach out below.

Contact email
studiofilm.app@gmail.com

Please include your device (e.g. iPhone 15) and a brief description of the issue — it helps us help you faster. We usually reply within a few days.

Frequently Asked Questions

I want to delete my account and videos

You can delete your account from Settings → Delete Account in the app. Once deleted, your account information and uploaded videos are removed without delay (except where retention is required by applicable law). See the Privacy Policy for details.

I can't sign in

Moafilm signs in with an Apple or Google account. If sign-in keeps failing, check your network connection first. If it still doesn't work, let us know which method you used (Apple/Google) at studiofilm.app@gmail.com.

Who can see the videos I upload?

Your videos aren't visible to anyone else until the Room they're in opens (is developed) at its set time. In a group Room you've invited friends to, your videos become visible to the other members once the Room opens. You can check the sharing scope before you upload.

I'd like to report a bug or share feedback

Use the feedback option in the app's Settings, or email us at the address above. Steps to reproduce the issue are a big help.
